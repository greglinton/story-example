Four neighborhoods have more than half of the total housing code cases

Figure 1: Mapping where most of the housing code violations are located

Out of the total 26,319 housing code cases, Asylum Hill, Barry Square, Northeast, and West End accounted for just over half the total number of cases with 13,186 or 50.10%. The remainder of the 13 neighborhoods constitute about 49.9% or 13,133 cases. Consistent with the earlier data, a targeted approach to reducing the number of housing code cases in the future would be an effective strategy.

41% of Housing code cases are still open in Hartford

As of 11/9/2021, out of the total 26,319 housing code cases, 10,860 were still open. That is about 41.26% of the cases. This is an alarmingly large proportion of cases. 58.73% or 15,459 cases were closed.
